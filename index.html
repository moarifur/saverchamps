About Us

Extra-curricular program designed to make financial literacy fun and engaging, giving your child valuable money skills

  • Exciting financial learning games
  • Humorous and colorful books
  • Fun money talks and conversations
  • Intuitive money learning activities and exercises
Benefits For Your Kids

Helping your child understand money, savings, and investment from their childhood to become financially independent and confident adult

  • Encourage saving and investment habits
  • Learn to spend money, smarter
  • Learn personal financial management
  • Learn about banking and accounts
  • Learn about the basics of investment
  • Learn about the basics of insurance
  • Learn the basics of tax

Experience-based real-life skills preparing your child to become a financially prudent person

  • Entrepreneurship development: Learn to earn
  • Budget: Learn to manage your expenditures
  • Savings: Learn the importance of saving money with Demo bank account
  • Investment: Learn investment with Demo investment and trading
  • Meet your Mentors: Connect and learn from real people in finance
  • Field trips: Banks and different money-learning places
